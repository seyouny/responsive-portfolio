About Me

Hello I'm Seyoung Yoon, but I mainly go by Stacey! I recently graduated with a Bachelor's in Chemistry from UCI. 2020 has been pretty rough so far, but we're all doing our best. I'm hoping to get a career in maybe software development, but I'm not too sure. I took a few Python classes, but I still feel like a beginner when it comes to development. I'm trying to pick up some new hobbies. I've learned how to longboard during this quarantine and I like to run. The thing I'm looking forward to the most when a vaccine comes out is hanging out with my friends, going to concerts, eating out at restaurants, and just going back to enjoying the outdoors. I listen to music 24/7 and would love some recommendations! Some of my favorite artists are Honne, Vansire, Still Woozy, Frank Ocean, Mac Miller, and RiceWine.

Some of my goals for the future is to get a small apartment and live with my future dog. Maybe somewhere close to the city so I can experience its night life to the fullest. I absolutely adore Joshua Tree-- the visible stars, the empty views, that feeling of being so small and vulnerable make me feel more human and humbled sometimes. It's a nice escape.
